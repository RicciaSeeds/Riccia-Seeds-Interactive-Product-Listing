Highest Germination Rates

It is very important to keep your seeds consistently moist until they germinate. Moist like a damp sponge. Also we do not recommend direct seeding of tomatoes, peppers, or brinjal (eggplant), as they are the hardest seeds to germinate. Please use a controlled environment like a seed starter tray, or the paper towel method (the method we use most often to test germination). Highest germination results best between 21.11° and 29.44° Celsius.
